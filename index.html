01

Who We Are

We are a community of passionate high schoolers with an interest in robotics. We design, build, program and test autonomous soccer robots to compete in Robocup Junior, a global STEM education initiative that involves over 110 countries and 4000 teams worldwide.

03

Workshops

We hold meetings every Saturday at Tigerlabs. Stop by if you want to get to know our teams, or want to get started in robotics. Additionally, we often host events at local libraries and Maker Faires. All skill levels are welcome!

If you are interested, give us a heads-up by filling out this form.
