Who We Are

We are a community of passionate high schoolers with an interest in robotics. We design, build, program and test autonomous soccer robots to compete in Robocup Junior, a global STEM education initiative that involves over 110 countries and 4000 teams worldwide.

Achievements

  • 2022
    • Elite: 4th Place World Robocup Championships, 1st Place National
    • Radian: 2nd Place National Robocup Junior Competition, Engineering Design Award
    • Orion: Best Poster Design Award at World Championships
  • 2021
    • Orion: 1st Place National Robocup Junior Competition
    • Elite: 4th Place at National Robocup Junior Competition
  • 2019
    • Orion: 1st Place National Robocup Junior Competition

Workshops

We hold meetings every other Saturday at Tigerlabs. Stop by if you want to get to know our teams, or want to get started in robotics. Additionally, we often host events at local libraries and Maker Faires. All skill levels are welcome!
